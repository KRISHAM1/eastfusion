WE ARE EAST FUSION

East Fusion is a tech startup company based in Uganda that specializes in providing innovative immersive tech solutions in various industries.

We leverage technologies like Extended Reality (XR), Artificial Intelligence (AI), and Hardware to create transformative experiences and solutions for our clients.

WHAT WE DO

ARCHITECTURE

We offer immersive tech solutions that allow architects and real estate developers to visualize and present their designs in a realistic and interactive manner. This includes virtual tours, 3D modeling, and augmented reality (AR) applications.

EDUCATION

We develop immersive educational experiences that enhance learning and engagement. Through virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR), we create interactive simulations, virtual field trips, and training programs for educational institutions.

TOURISM

We provide immersive tech solutions for the tourism industry, enabling travelers to explore destinations virtually before visiting them physically. Our VR and AR applications offer interactive travel guides, virtual tours, and immersive experiences for tourists.

REAL ESTATE

We enhance the real estate buying and selling process by offering immersive experiences. Through VR and AR, potential buyers can virtually tour properties, visualize renovations, and experience interactive property showcases.

MARKETING

We help businesses create impactful marketing campaigns by utilizing immersive technologies. Our solutions include VR and AR experiences for product launches, interactive advertisements, and engaging brand experiences.

AUTOMOTIVE

We revolutionize the automotive industry by leveraging virtual showrooms, interactive car configurators, and AR-based maintenance and repair guides, you can enhance the customer experience and provide valuable assistance to car owners.
